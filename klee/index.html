Description

「超時空BB蛋」是一個快節奏的平台跳躍動作遊戲。用簡單的虛擬按鍵操作配合自動射擊，清空遊戲畫面吧！喔還有別忘了回收重要的$能量核心$，解除世界的能源危機！

Klee is a fast-paced arcade game. Clean up game rooms with your auto-blaster guns and simple virtual button control! Oh, and don't forget to recycle the stabilized $energy_cores$ to save the world!
