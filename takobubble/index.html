Game Story

Octopus Tako's beetle collection was blown away by a magical swirl of bubbles! Could you help it pop all the bubbles and find them back?

Description

Tako Bubble is a turn-based casual puzzler. You play as Tako, a yellow octopus, to crawl through deep-ocean environments, defeat ferocious monsters, pop all the bubbles, and find hidden beetle gems.
